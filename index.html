    ❓ frequently asked questions

    How often should I test my soil?

    For most home gardens, once per year in early spring is sufficient. For intensive vegetable production, test after each crop cycle to track nutrient drawdown.

    What’s the best mulch for moisture retention?

    Straw and leaf mold offer excellent moisture retention while adding organic matter. Avoid fresh wood chips near annuals – they can tie up nitrogen.

    Can I use coffee grounds directly in the garden?

    Yes, but moderate: coffee grounds are slightly acidic and nitrogen-rich. Mix with browns (leaves, cardboard) to balance carbon‑nitrogen ratio.
