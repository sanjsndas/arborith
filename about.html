Moss canopy values

🌿 Soil as system

We see soil not as dirt, but as a living community. Every recommendation starts with biology — fungi, bacteria, and the invisible web.

🧭 Climate humility

Gardening in shifting zones demands adaptability. We champion resilient perennials, water-wise design, and observation over force.

📚 Open knowledge

All our guides, data, and protocols are shared without paywalls. Gardening intelligence should be a commons, not a commodity.

🕊️ Slow craft

From grafting to compost, we honor the pace of seasons. No shortcuts — only patient, deliberate practice rooted in evidence.

Our story

📷 the team · field notes, 2020

From a single raised bed to a global community

Arborith started in a rain-soaked garden in the Willamette Valley. What began as a personal log of soil temperatures and cover crop trials grew into a digital library — now visited by growers in 80+ countries.

2018 First article: “Mycorrhizae in disturbed soils” — 200 reads.
2020 Launched the “Canopy Notes” newsletter; reached 5k subscribers.
2022 Published the open-access “Regenerative Bed Prep” series, used by schools and extension programs.
2025 Arborith now hosts 400+ articles, 30+ video tutorials, and a collaborative phenology map.
